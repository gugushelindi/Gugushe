Mosadi wa Matsoho (The woman and her Hands)

I am a 25 year old Black African Woman, who is passionate about visual communication. My life journey so far, have determined my career choices and interests. I have an honors degree in BSc Urban and Reginal Planning. I am aiming to channel my career into the creative industry. I fell in love with drawing in my childhood and pursuing the great art of creative thinking in my adulthood. In my years at university I developed the love for digital design and capitalised on my skills by helping start-up companies with their visual communication. My mission is to work toward becoming a Creative Director. My style of work is minimal and expresses the simpliest form of visual communication.
